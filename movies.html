A Taxi Driver

Action drama about a German journalist and a Korean taxi driver who drive to Gwangju to cover the 1980 uprising.

The End of Evangelion

Sci-Fi mecha with a lot of psychological elements and symbolism. Very artistic/avant garde.

A Silent Voice

Drama about a high schooler who attempts to redeem himself after bullying a deaf girl in elementary school. Sadge.
